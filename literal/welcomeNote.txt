Welcome to this image repository!
You can:
	Create an account; if you do not wish to do so, you can also upload your pictures anonymously.
	Add your own image; make sure it is uploaded first. 
	Search for images on the repo. You may search by the image's name, which is not unique.
typing the following commands in []:
	[ACCOUNT]	create your account and start selling your pictures
	[ADD]		add a picture to the repo
	[SEARCH]	Search a picture by a name
	[ACCINFO]	see your account information
